All Alrouf products are covered by a limited warranty.

1. Standard warranty period:
   - Streetlights: 1 year from delivery.
   - Bollards and decorative lights: 2 years from delivery.

2. Warranty covers:
   - Manufacturing defects.
   - Electrical component failures not caused by misuse.

3. Warranty excludes:
   - Damage due to improper installation, accident, or alteration.
   - Normal wear and tear or exposure to extreme weather beyond rated limits.

4. To claim warranty, clients must provide:
   - Proof of purchase and delivery date.
   - Photographs of the damaged unit.
